Why tabbi

Every item accounted for

Most split apps make you guess portions or divide everything evenly. tabbi reads the actual receipt and lets you assign each line item to whoever ordered it.

Reads the receipt for you

Photograph the check and tabbi pulls out every line item — no typing, no squinting at faded thermal paper.

Drag items to people

Assign dishes with a drag. Shared appetizers? Drop the same item on multiple people and tabbi splits the cost.

Tax and tip included

Each person sees a final number before anyone reaches for their wallet. No calculator, no "I'll Venmo you later."
