Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how this application (“the App”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use it.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve the App:

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Account identifiers associated with your profile

Contact Information (User-Selected)

The App allows you to select a contact from your device to represent yourself within the App. We may collect and store:

  • Name of the selected contact
  • Phone number and/or other contact details associated with that selected contact (if provided by the device and chosen by the user)

We only access the contact that you explicitly select for use in the App.

Feature Requests and User Submissions

If you submit a feature request or other feedback through the App, we collect:

  • The content of your submission
  • Associated account information

Subscription and Purchase Information

We collect information about your subscription status, such as:

  • Whether you are on a free or paid plan
  • Subscription status and entitlement information

We do not collect or store payment card information.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Provide core app functionality
  • Enable the contact-based features of the App
  • Process and manage subscriptions and paywall access
  • Respond to feature requests and improve the App
  • Maintain security and prevent misuse

3. Artificial Intelligence

Our app uses artificial intelligence to provide certain features and responses. Information submitted to these features may be processed by our AI service providers solely to deliver the requested functionality. We do not use your information to train our own AI models.

4. Payments and Subscriptions

All payments and in-app purchases are processed by Apple through Apple’s In-App Purchase system.

Subscription management and entitlement verification are handled by RevenueCat.

We do not collect or store any payment card details.

5. How We Store and Process Data

Your information is stored and processed using Supabase, which acts as our backend database provider.

Data may include:

  • Account information
  • Selected contact information
  • Subscription status
  • Feature requests

All data is transmitted securely using encryption (HTTPS).

6. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information.

We only share information with third-party service providers necessary to operate the App, including:

  • Supabase (data storage and backend services)
  • RevenueCat (subscription management)
  • Apple (in-app purchase processing)

These providers only process data as needed to provide their services.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services.

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent where applicable

To exercise these rights, contact us using the information below.

9. Contact Permissions

The App requests permission to access a contact selected by you from your device.

This access is used solely to:

  • Enable the App’s core functionality involving your selected contact

We do not access your full contact list.

10. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

11. Feature Requests

Users may submit feature requests or feedback within the App. These submissions may be stored and reviewed to improve the App.

12. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the “Last updated” date above.

14. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at:
